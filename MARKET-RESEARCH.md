## 6. Regulations & Certifications

### State & Local Licensing Requirements

- **No statewide cleaning license required** -- Florida DBPR does not regulate cleaning as a licensed profession
- **Business Registration**: Must register business entity (LLC for $125, corporation for $70) via Sunbiz.org
- **Business Tax Receipt (BTR)**: Required in most counties/cities (Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach each have their own requirements)
- **Sales Tax Permit**: Mandatory for commercial cleaning services (free online registration)
- **Certificate of Use**: May be required depending on local jurisdiction
- **EIN**: Required if employing staff
- **DBA/Fictitious Name**: Required if operating under a trade name

### Insurance Requirements

| Insurance Type | Purpose | Priority |
|---------------|---------|----------|
| **General Liability** | Covers property damage, bodily injury to third parties | MANDATORY |
| **Workers' Compensation** | Covers employee injuries on the job | MANDATORY (with employees) |
| **Surety Bond** | Protects clients against theft or incomplete work | HIGHLY RECOMMENDED |
| **Commercial Auto** | Covers vehicles used in business operations | REQUIRED for company vehicles |
| **Umbrella/Excess Liability** | Additional coverage above standard limits | RECOMMENDED for large contracts |

### Industry Certifications (Competitive Advantage)

| Certification | Issuing Body | What It Proves | Contract Impact |
|--------------|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **ISSA CIMS** | ISSA | Operational excellence: quality systems, service delivery, HR, safety, sustainability | Gold standard for winning bids; valid 2 years with assessor review |
| **CIMS-GB (Green Building)** | ISSA | LEED compliance and eco-friendly practices | Required for LEED-certified buildings |
| **CIMS Advanced by GBAC** | ISSA/GBAC | Infectious disease prevention, disinfection protocols, biorisk response | Post-pandemic requirement for many healthcare/institutional clients |
| **GBAC STAR** | GBAC | Hygiene, infection prevention, sustainability | Major trust signal for medical/educational facilities |
| **IICRC** | IICRC | Carpet cleaning, restoration, and water damage expertise | Essential for carpet cleaning credibility |
| **OSHA Compliance** | OSHA | Workplace safety and hazard communication | Federal requirement; demonstrates professionalism |

### Certifications That Help Win Larger Contracts

1. **ISSA CIMS (especially Advanced by GBAC)** -- the single most impactful certification for winning commercial bids
2. **GBAC STAR** -- critical for healthcare, education, and government contracts
3. **IICRC** -- essential for carpet cleaning and restoration credibility
4. **BBB Accreditation** -- trust signal that property managers actively check
5. **Green Seal / LEED Compliance** -- increasingly required for Class A office buildings
