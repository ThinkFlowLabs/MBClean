# MB Clean Solutions -- Conversion & Content Strategy

## Key Conversion Benchmarks

- Cleaning services achieve **17-23% paid search conversion rates** and **15-17% organic** (far above 3.3% industry average)
- Page load: 1-second pages have **2.5x higher conversion** than 5-second pages; each 1-second delay = -7% CR
- Mobile drives **60.9% of traffic**; **78% of local searches convert within 24 hours**
- Phone calls convert at **27.15%** for cleaning/janitorial -- dramatically higher than web forms (2-3%)

## Trust Signal Impact

A cleaning company CRO case study showed **83% sitewide conversion rate increase** (2.01% to 3.68%) and **90% mobile uplift** after amplifying trust signals -- without any traffic growth.

### Trust Signals Ranked by Impact
1. Customer reviews/testimonials (especially video)
2. Client logos (recognizable brands)
3. Case studies with specific metrics
4. Certifications (ISSA CIMS, Green Seal, GBAC)
5. Insurance/bonding displays
6. Satisfaction guarantees
7. Years in business
8. Real staff photos (not stock)

## CTA Strategy

### Best-Performing CTAs
- Personalized CTAs convert **200% better** than generic text
- Below-fold CTAs after value explanation = **220-304% boost**
- Above-fold CTAs get **102% more views**
- Recommendation: **3-5 CTAs per page** with varied phrasing + sticky phone bar on mobile

### CTA Copy That Converts for Cleaning
| CTA | Use Case |
|-----|----------|
| "Get Your Free Estimate" | Primary hero |
| "Call Now: (XXX) XXX-XXXX" | Sticky header + mobile |
| "See What We Can Do" | After before/after gallery |
| "Get a Quote in 60 Seconds" | Form sections |
| "Schedule Your Free Walkthrough" | Service pages |
| "View Our Work" | Portfolio/gallery sections |

## Homepage Structure (Optimized for Conversion)

### Recommended 7-Section Layout
1. **Hero** with headline + CTA + phone number (above fold)
2. **Trust bar** -- certifications, client logos, star ratings
3. **Services overview** -- 6-9 service cards with icons
4. **Why choose us** -- 3-4 differentiators with proof
5. **How it works** -- 3-step process
6. **Case studies / before-after** -- Visual proof
7. **Final CTA / quote form** -- Lead capture

### Key Specs
- 800 words max on homepage
- 50% visual content
- Under 3 seconds load time
- Dual messaging: property managers (scalability, compliance) AND small business owners (flexibility, affordability)

## Differentiation Formula

**Personality + Proof + Ease = Standout Service Website**

From case studies of successful "boring" service businesses:
- **Personality**: Brand voice, custom photography, humor, unique visual style
- **Proof**: Real case studies, named projects, before/after with metrics
- **Ease**: Simple quote process, transparent pricing, instant response

## Lead Capture Strategy

### Multi-Step Form (Best Practice)
Step 1: Service type (dropdown/buttons)
Step 2: Property details (size, frequency)
Step 3: Contact info (name, phone, email)

Multi-step forms outperform single-page forms significantly.

### Instant Quote Calculator
The "money-maker" for cleaning companies. Let users input:
- Square footage
- Service type
- Frequency
- Get an estimated range instantly

### Chatbot/Live Chat
- Hybrid model: AI chatbot for after-hours, live agent during business
- Recommended platforms: Voiceflow, ZenMaid, Housecall Pro
