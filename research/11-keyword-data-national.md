## AI/LLM SEO Insights

The AI Optimization keyword data returned results for all 7 target keywords, confirming these terms are being searched in LLM contexts. This means:

1. **LLMs are being asked about commercial cleaning services** -- MB Clean needs to be mentioned in AI-generated results
2. **Key strategy**: Create comprehensive, authoritative content that LLMs will reference
3. **Focus areas for LLM SEO**:
   - Detailed service descriptions with specific processes explained
   - Location-specific content with real addresses, service areas, phone numbers
   - Client testimonials and case studies with measurable results
   - FAQ pages answering common questions in conversational format
   - Structured data (Schema.org LocalBusiness, Service, Review markup)
