# South Florida Commercial Cleaning -- Competitor Website Analysis

## 8 Competitors Analyzed

## Common Patterns Across All Competitors

### Layout
- **Hero with integrated lead form** -- Most effective pattern
- **Phone number in nav** -- Universal
- **Industry/facility segmentation** -- All organize by facility type (offices, healthcare, schools, condos)
- **Service area pages** -- Dedicated pages per city/county
- **Trust signal footer** -- Badges, certifications, logos clustered at bottom
- **Client logo carousels** -- Prominent social proof

### Imagery
- Uniformed staff actively working
- Clean commercial interior shots
- Dark overlays on hero with white text
- Certification/badge graphics prominent
- Before/after photos rare but effective

### Color Distribution
| Approach | Used By | Best For |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Blue-Forward | Ace Cleaning | Trust, corporate clients |
| Green-Forward | SparkleTeam, Service Keepers | Eco positioning |
| Navy + Orange | TamCare | Standing out, energy + stability |
| Teal + Navy | AK Building | Modern, tech-forward |
| Dark/Premium | Total Cleaning | High-end differentiation |

---

## Differentiating USPs (Less Common, More Effective)
1. **Proprietary technology/apps** -- Quality control and communication
2. **Veteran/woman-owned** -- Meaningful in government contracts
3. **Pricing transparency** -- Very rare, very effective
4. **Thought leadership** -- President featured in publications
5. **Case studies with named projects** -- Real proof
6. **2-hour guaranteed response time** -- Concrete promise
7. **Bilingual content** -- Massively underutilized in South Florida's diverse market

---

## Key Opportunities for MB Clean Solutions

1. **Hero with embedded lead form** -- Copy the pattern that works
2. **Break the green/blue convention** with distinctive colors
3. **Bilingual (English/Spanish)** -- Almost no competitor does this in Miami
4. **Before/after galleries** -- Rare among competitors, highly effective
5. **Pricing transparency** -- Only Polaris does this, major differentiator
6. **Real photography** -- Uniformed team, actual work sites
7. **Technology/app messaging** even if simple (online booking, digital reports)
8. **Terrazzo/marble specialization** -- No competitor owns this niche in their messaging
