### Fort Lauderdale Key Insights
- **"commercial cleaning fort lauderdale" at $0.94 CPC is a GOLDMINE** -- confirmed volume of 210/mo with absurdly low CPC compared to Miami ($8.35 CPC). This means fewer advertisers are competing for this keyword.

- **Miami Top Cleaning has NO dedicated Fort Lauderdale pages** -- only a navigation link to "Fort Lauderdale" as a served area. This is a massive gap MB Clean can exploit with dedicated, content-rich pages.

## 11. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### 1. Fort Lauderdale is the #1 City to Target After Miami
- 210/mo confirmed for "commercial cleaning fort lauderdale" at just $0.94 CPC
- 140/mo confirmed for "office cleaning fort lauderdale" at $3.55 CPC
- Miami Top Cleaning has NO real Fort Lauderdale pages -- just a nav link
- Build a rich Fort Lauderdale location hub with service sub-pages

### 2. Pressure Washing Keywords Dominate Volume
- Across all cities, pressure washing/cleaning keywords have 2-3x the volume of commercial cleaning keywords
- MB Clean should ensure pressure washing gets a prominent, SEO-optimized service page
- Create city-specific pressure washing pages for Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton, Hollywood, and West Palm Beach

### 3. Broward County Is the Biggest Untapped Market
- Miami Top Cleaning has ZERO Broward County coverage beyond a Fort Lauderdale nav link
- Most national franchises focus on Miami-Dade
- Building a Broward County hub page + individual city pages gives MB Clean a first-mover advantage

### 4. "Pressure Cleaning" vs "Pressure Washing" -- Use Both
- Florida locals often say "pressure cleaning" rather than "pressure washing"
- "Pressure washing" has higher national search volume
- Use "pressure washing" as primary keyword, "pressure cleaning" in body copy and secondary headings

### 5. Palm Beach = Low Volume but Easy Wins
- Very low competition -- almost no one is actively targeting Palm Beach cleaning keywords
- Worth building but AFTER Fort Lauderdale and Broward are established
- Use "west palm beach" not "palm beach" for better volume targeting

### 6. Doral and Coral Gables Need Competitive Pages
- Miami Top Cleaning already has dedicated pages for both cities
- MB Clean must match or exceed their content depth
- Doral: emphasize warehouse/industrial cleaning
- Coral Gables: emphasize marble/terrazzo (upscale properties)
