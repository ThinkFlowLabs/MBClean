## CROSS-PAGE ANALYSIS & COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE

### Strengths of Miami Top Cleaning's Web Presence

1. **Comprehensive Service Pages** - Individual pages for every sub-service (12+ pages analyzed)
2. **SEO Structure** - Schema markup on every page (Product, Review, BreadcrumbList, WebPage, FAQPage)
3. **Keyword Targeting** - City name "Miami" in every H1
4. **Content Depth** - Carpet cleaning and concrete polishing pages are particularly thorough
5. **FAQ Sections** - Several pages use FAQ format for additional SEO value
6. **Breadcrumb Navigation** - Clear hierarchy (Home > Category > Sub-service)
7. **Google Translate** - English/Spanish bilingual support
8. **Multiple CTAs** - "Get Quote" button + contact form + phone + email on every page

### Weaknesses & Opportunities for MB Clean Solutions

1. **Same 3 Testimonials Everywhere** - Only 3 reviews recycled across all 12 pages. MB can show service-specific testimonials.
2. **No Pricing Transparency** - Only 1 page out of 12 shows any pricing ($0.50/sq ft for final cleaning). MB can win with transparent pricing.
3. **Generic Images** - Many pages have only 1-2 stock-looking images. MB can use real job photos.
4. **No Before/After Photos** - Zero before/after comparisons anywhere. Major opportunity for MB.
5. **No Video Content** - No embedded videos or process demonstrations.
6. **No Industry Certifications Displayed** - Claims "licensed and insured" but shows no ISSA, IICRC, BSCAI, or other certification badges.
7. **No Team Photos/Bios** - Mentions team but no staff introductions or expertise profiles.
8. **COVID-19 Still Prominent** - Disinfecting page still heavily COVID-focused in 2026, feeling dated.
9. **Same Contact Form Everywhere** - No service-specific form fields or scheduling tools.
10. **No Online Booking/Scheduling** - Only contact form and phone.
11. **No Case Studies** - No detailed project stories or facility transformations.
12. **No Service Area Maps** - Only text listing of areas served.
13. **Weak CTA Variety** - "Get Quote" is the only button CTA; no "Schedule Free Assessment" or "See Our Work" alternatives.
14. **No Trust Badges** - No BBB, Chamber of Commerce, industry association badges.
15. **No Guarantee Language** - No satisfaction guarantee, except implied in carpet page.
16. **Limited Internal Linking** - Related services links exist but are basic.
17. **No Blog Content Visible on Service Pages** - Blog exists but not integrated into service pages.
