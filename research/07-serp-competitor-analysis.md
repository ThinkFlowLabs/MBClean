# SERP Competitor Analysis: Commercial Cleaning Keywords -- Miami/South Florida

## Executive Summary

Analysis of actual search engine results for 10 commercial cleaning keywords in the Miami/South Florida market. Identifies who ranks, their strategies, and where MB Clean Solutions can win.

**Critical finding**: MB Clean Solutions already ranks on page 1 for 3 keywords:
- "commercial cleaning services miami" -- Position 10
- "floor waxing service miami" -- Position 6
- "commercial air duct cleaning miami" -- Position 8

---

## Easiest Wins (Weakest SERPs)

### 1. "terrazzo cleaning miami" -- WEAKEST SERP
- A Facebook post and equipment rental company rank on page 1
- A Squarespace site ranks at #7
- Well-optimized page could crack top 3 within weeks

### 2. "marble polishing miami" -- WEAK SERP
- Small sites with thin content dominate
- Yelp is #1 (directory)
- Exact-match domain (marblepolishingmiami.com) ranks twice
- No national franchises present

### 3. "commercial air duct cleaning miami" -- MODERATE
- Exact-match domains dominate but have poor site quality
- MB Solutions already at #8

---

## Toughest Keywords

### "commercial cleaning services miami" -- HARDEST
- National franchises (Jani-King, Vanguard, ABM, Enviro-Master)
- Strong locals (miamitopcleaning.com, 305janitorialservices.com)
- MB Solutions at #10 (still on page 1!)

### "tile and grout cleaning miami" -- HARD
- Franchise specialists: Grout Doctor, Groutsmith, Sir Grout
- Niche authority + national backlink profiles

---

## The Dominant Competitor: miamitopcleaning.com

Appears on page 1 for **6 of 10 keywords**, #1 in 4 of them.

**Their strategy:**
- Individual service pages for every offering
- Consistent "Service + Miami" title tag pattern
- Phone number in meta descriptions
- Professional SEO campaign (documented by OnTheMap agency)
- Strong internal linking between service pages
- Shifted from residential to commercial keyword targeting

**Service pages:**
- /floor-care/waxing-stripping/
- /floor-care/carpet-cleaning/
- /floor-care/terrazzo-commercial-cleaning-and-polishing/
- /construction/
- /pressure-cleaning/
- /industrial-cleaning-services/

## Winning Title Tag Patterns
1. `[Service] + [City] | [Brand]` -- Most common
2. `[Adjective] + [Service] + in [City], FL | [Brand]` -- Franchise pattern
3. `[Brand]: [Service] + [City]` -- Brand-first
4. `[Action] + [Service] | [Brand]` -- CTA-oriented
5. `Best/Top + [Service] + in [City] | [Brand]` -- Superlative

## Winning Meta Description Elements
- Phone number for click-to-call
- "Free Quote" or "Free Estimate"
- List of specific services
- Service area mentions (Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach)
- Trust signals: "Licensed & Insured", years experience
- Clear CTA
