## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This keyword strategy targets commercial/B2B cleaning clients across South Florida's three major counties. The market is competitive but heavily fragmented, with most competitors failing to fully optimize for all service-specific and location-specific keyword combinations. The biggest opportunities lie in:

- **Terrazzo and marble polishing** - High value, lower competition, very relevant to South Florida's architecture
- **Post-construction cleaning** - Growing demand with new development activity
- **Floor stripping and waxing** - High commercial intent, moderate competition
- **Air duct cleaning** - Strong volume nationally (165,000/mo for "air duct cleaning"), with commercial-specific variants underserved
- **VCT floor care** - Niche commercial term with very low competition

Key insight: South Florida has a unique advantage for marble and terrazzo services due to the prevalence of these materials in the region's commercial buildings, condos, and lobbies. This is a differentiator to exploit heavily.

## 7. CONTENT STRATEGY RECOMMENDATIONS

Each service page should include:
1. **H1** with primary keyword + location (e.g., "Commercial Floor Stripping & Waxing in South Florida")
2. **Introduction** (150-200 words) explaining the service with natural keyword integration
3. **H2 sections** for each sub-service or process step
4. **Cities served section** listing all Tier 1-3 cities (important for local SEO)
5. **FAQ section** with schema markup (targets "People Also Ask" results)
6. **Before/after gallery** with alt text containing keywords
7. **CTA** with phone number and form
8. **Testimonials** from commercial clients
